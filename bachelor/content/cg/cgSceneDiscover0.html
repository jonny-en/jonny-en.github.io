First: The Model

3D models are defined through geometric ratios between points, lines and areas.
Building these models is a part of both, our courses at the university and our work in the group. Of course they can be extremely complex, but in this example it's just a simple sphere.
Try playing with the parameters to change the size and the number of segments used displaying the sphere.
