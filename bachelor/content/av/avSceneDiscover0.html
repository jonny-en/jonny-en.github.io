Movement Detection

Every picture a camera takes is represented by a multidude of little pixels, each with its very own color. To simply detect motion, the program that runs in the background has to figure out, whether the pixels at a specific location has changed regarding their mean value.

In this small example of movement detection you can control Lisa with moving your finger over the particular field. on the webcam image.

NOTICE: You need to activate your webcam. Make sure to accept the browsers usage of your camera on this site.
