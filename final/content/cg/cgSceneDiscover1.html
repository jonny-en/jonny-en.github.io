Second: Let there be light!

The screens color of every pixel displaying the sphere is the result of a more ore less complicated formula that more often than not includes the light that hits an object. In our working group we're focusing on real-time capable ray tracing, which creates an ultra realistic and physically correct image. To get an idea of how lightning works, try to play with the parameters of the lights shining on the sphere.



In the next step the spheres are going to look like what they are supposed to be: planets.
