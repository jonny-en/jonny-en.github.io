OBJECT TRACKING

Have you ever wondered why cameras are able to recognise objects such as cars, people and faces?
Like always there are a lot of different ways to get what you want, but in the case of object recognition every one of them starts with a very important step: The preprocessing.
The preprocessing itself can involve a high number of single steps or filters, which are optimizing the incoming picture of the camera for future calculations.

In this example, Lisa's camera is able to show some of these filters. You can choose and combine them on the right side of the page, to separate the obects.
NOTICE: The outcoming result highly depends on the order in which the filters are chosen.
